OUR MENU

Artisanal doughnuts

It started with the cronut, the unbelievably delicious croissant-doughnut hybrid popularized by NYC pastry chef Dominique Ansel. Now, specialty doughnut shops are popping up all over the country, and your event could follow suit. But don’t limit yourself — while it’s among the most popular breakfast catering ideas, it’s also a great choice when you’re simply looking for fun catering options.

Smoothie bowl bar

These beautiful breakfast bowls typically consist of a mix of fruits, veggies, oats, yogurt, and “superfoods” like acai, chia, and hemp. A perfect choice if you want a range of healthy catering menu ideas or even easy catering ideas that don’t require a lot of dishes or utensils. Serve them in recyclable bowls for even quicker cleanup.

Rolled ice cream parlor

The frozen dessert originally from Southeast Asia is also sometimes called “stir-fried ice cream”. Think delicious gelato, flattened on a sheet and then rolled into a vertical cylinder. Add toppings, stick a spoon in it, and bam! You’ve got yourself a delicious — and highly Instagrammable — festival treat.
